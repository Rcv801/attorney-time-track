*   **LeanLaw (Anti‑Clio Score: 8/10):** Proves the billing-only market exists at venture scale. However, it's locked to QuickBooks and priced above our target sweet spot at $55/month.
*   **TimeNet Law (Anti‑Clio Score: 9/10):** The closest spiritual match. Proves a solo dev can sustain a privacy-first, indie product for decades. Limited by being Mac-only with no web/mobile presence.
*   **Time59 (Anti‑Clio Score: 7/10):** Proves that an ultra-affordable ($<40/month) model works for solos, validating price sensitivity in the market.

### **Strategic Implications for Us**

We can fill the whitespace by combining the best of all three: LeanLaw's billing focus, TimeNet's privacy-first architecture, Time59's affordability, and our own unique AI features (like billing narrative generation and passive time capture).
